Simple Waffle Recipe

Source: petitchef

7 servings

  • 350 gr (3 cups) flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 56 gr (3 tbsp) sugar
  • 56 gr (0.5 cup) butter
  • 560 ml (2 cups) milk
  • 1.5 tps vanilla extract
  1. Take the egg yolks from the whites. Mix the yolks with the flour, the sugar and the vanilla extract.
  2. Gradually add the milk, mixing well to avoid lumps. Then, add the melted butter.
  3. Whisk your egg whites until they form peaks. Add them to the batter, gently, without crushing them. Put the batter in the fridge for 1 hour.
  4. Apply some oil on your waffle iron, and let it get hot. Pour 2 laddles of the batter, and close the iron. Leave it to cook for 4 minutes.
  5. There you are, your waffles are ready!
