Dough

Source Pingo Doce

Ingredients

  • Water (110ml)
  • Sugar (1 coffee spoon)
  • Fresh baker yeast (10g)
  • Flour (200g)
  • Olive oil (10ml)
  • Salt (1 coffee spoon)

Preparation

  1. Put the water, sugar and yeast in a bowl.
    Gently fold until the yeast is diluted.
  2. Add the oil, salt and floud.
    Knead with your hands until you get a fluffy and homogeneous dough.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and set aside at room temperature until doubled in volume.
  4. Place the dough on a bench sprinkled with flour, and it is ready to roll out in the desired way.

Rest of the pizza

Base

    Cut garlic in very small pieces, add a dash of olive oil and turn on the stove. Let it braise.
    Join tomato pulp, add a little of sugar and mix it.

Stuff on the pizza

I usually add salami/tuna, peach, pineapple and mushrooms

When finished, cover with cheese. IMO closing the pizza as a calzone makes it better.
